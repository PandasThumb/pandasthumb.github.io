Happy Lamarck day

By Joe Felsenstein
August 1, 2025 13:00 MST 0 Comments

 

Once again, it is the birthday (August 1st) of Jean Baptiste Pierre Antoine de Monet, the Chevalier de Lamarck. He was the first major evolutionary biologist (as far as I am concerned). He drew some of the first evolutionary trees, and suggested mechanisms for evolution, including adaptation. (His mechanisms were wrong, of course). He was also a major pioneer of invertebrate biology.

On past birthdays I have covered many aspects of Lamarck’s life and work, down to and including the avenue and Paris Metro station named after him. This time, allow me to digress to the life of his patron Buffon, who also has a claim to be an early evolutionist.
