The photographer notes that feeders apparently encourage overwintering of some migratory birds such as this violet-crowned hummingbird, which was photographed on February 23 of this year. We see similar behavior among Canada geese (<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada_goose"><i>Branta canadensis</i></a>) in Boulder, Colorado, where Dr. Dullien and I both live; she comments on "how new food sources can alter bird migration patterns causing some species to forgo migration." </figcaption>
