Yikes! A newish follower of PT, Robert Dullien, has just notified us that today is <a href="https://www.wwf.org.uk/learn/world-days/national-panda-day">National Panda Day</a>. You can celebrate it by following the link above to the World Wildlife Federation's National Panda Day website. The WWF invites you as follows:

<blockquote><p><strong>ON THIS DAY WE CELEBRATE PANDAS!</strong></p>
<p>To coincide with National Panda Day, we’d like to invite you to get to know more about this amazing species. We've got some simple and fun ways to show your support; from learning more about pandas, to baking a panda cake!</p></blockquote>

Activities include an article on how the panda was possibly saved from extinction, to a quiz, top 10 facts about pandas, a drawing by Andy Robert Davies to be colored by a child, young or old, and more.

Our thanks to Mr. Dullien for being so alert!
